## 👨‍💻 About Me

I'm a developer who enjoys understanding **how things work behind the scenes**.

I love taking an idea from a blank project folder to a fully deployed application. Whether it's designing APIs, building frontends, deploying servers, or automating workflows, I enjoy every part of the process.

Most of what I know comes from building projects, making mistakes, debugging for hours, and improving one step at a time.

Outside of writing code, I'm always exploring better ways to write cleaner software, automate repetitive work, and learn new technologies.

> **Building, breaking, fixing, and learning — that's pretty much my workflow.**
